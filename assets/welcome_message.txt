ğŸ’¡ <b>What is Homework for Life?</b>

As Matthew Dicks explains:

<i>"Homework for Life is designed to help you find stories in the every day of your life. It takes about five minutes a day."</i>

The practice is beautifully simple:

<i>"At the end of every day, reflect upon your day and ask yourself one simple question: If I had to tell a story from today - a five-minute story onstage about something that took place over the course of this day - what would it be?"</i>

Why does this matter?

<i>"When you start looking for story-worthy moments in your life, you start to see them everywhere... Homework for Life has a way of making every single day more meaningful."</i>
